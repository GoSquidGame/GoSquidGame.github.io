  • Ojingeo Bonus:

Project Concept and Vision

  • The Squid Game Card NFT project aims to build a decentralized blockchain based Squid Game Metaverse by fairly distributing NFT and ERC20 token, SQUID, around the community.
  • We want to provide a suitable technical environment and community fund for development teams that want to provide applications such as games, Gamified DeFi, Community, and Commerce, which are components of the Squid Game Metaverse.
  • New projects are discovered and financially supported based on the community to create a decentralized game ecosystem
  • It not only distributes all economic resources fairly, but also ensures long-term dispersion and balance.
  • The consonants of ‘Hangul’, invented to express all sounds, are used as the symbol system for the building blocks of the metaverse.
  • It provides various modular building blocks for developing secure and flexible decentralized games and services, and Metaverse UX.

What is the Squid game card NFT ?

  • ‘Squid Game Card’ is an NFT for infrastructure elements of Metaverse using blockchain.
  • The ‘Squid Game Card’ has 3 Hangul consonant properties and 8 numeric properties.
  • When you mint or purchase this card, it means that you are invited to the Squid Game Metaverse ecosystem.

What do the 'Korean consonants' and 8 number attributes on the squid game card mean?

  • It has no predefined meaning. It can be used in various meanings depending on the creative interpretation of the game's developers and users.
  • It is intentionally designed not to give any meaning to the attributes in order to promote endless creative imagination.

How can I get the ‘Squid Game Card’?

  • You can participate in the 20 rounds of Free Minting Round and pay the gas fee and mint it on [Free Minting] menu or the web3 page of the squid game card smart contract for free.
  • Users who are willing to buy the ‘Squid Game Card’ can obtain it by purchasing a card in OpenSea(https://opensea.io/collection/squid-game-card-nft).

Can I still issue squid game cards for free?

  • Yes! Since Free minting rounds of the Squid Game card are not over yet, anyone can mint it for free.
  • For detailed minting method, refer to [Free Minting] page.

Can I mint several at once?

  • No! Only one squid game card can be minted at a time on [Free Minting] menu or Etherscan's squid game smart contract web3 page. There is no limit to the number of cards that one person can mint.

After minting or purchasing a Squid Game card, where can I view it?

  • Since the Squid Game Card NFT is registered on the OpenSea Marketplace, you can check the minted or purchased Squid Game card in the [Profile] menu of your account by connecting the wallet such as the metamask used for issuing it to the OpenSea site.

Where is the 'Squid Game Card' used?

  • In the Squid Metaverse, use with an 'invitation ticket' for dApps that require 'specific card possession' as a condition of participation, or
  • It can be combined with other smart contracts to transform or transform it into an 'item for a specific purpose', or
  • It will be used to exchange tokens owned by other users within the Squid Metaverse.

Can I create a game or community with the 'Squid Game Card'?

  • Of course. For users who have 'Squid Game Card', you can create your own game or community application by independently assigning qualitative characteristics to the attributes assigned to 'Korean consonants' and 'numbers' of the 'Squid Game Card'.

If only 9,576 are minted, isn't it difficult for most people to have a squid game card?

  • The squid game expansion cards can be issued as an independent smart contract according to the needs of games or communities.
  • The 0th generation squid game card minted in step 1 is designed to issue a new squid game card of the next generation in the way of 'Breeding' from this.

Are the 11 attributes given to the squid game card enough?

  • 11 attributes are enough to form a variety of combinations, but if more attributes are needed, new attributes can be added using the base seed (gene information) included in each NFT card.

How is the value of the Squid Game Card determined?

  • The value of the Squid Game card is not inherent in itself, but is given by the game or community created using this card.
  • However, since some attributes given to the squid game card are issued with their ‘rarity’ adjusted during the issuance stage, the frequency of attribute expression can be a factor that fundamentally affects value formation.

Oops. It's a surprise! I’m really frustrated at the huge gas fee to claim a card

  • One of the reasons why Etherscan shows abnormally high gas fees is that new cards cannot be claimed at that time (we call it ‘round break’).
  • 20 rounds of 456 card claims are being executed successively but there are 6 hours of round break between two rounds. Check the ‘Countdown’ at the top of the home page to see when the next round starts.

If I draw a ‘○△□’ card, can I win 1 ETH prize?

  • No. A card is randomly selected out of 456 cards in each round and if the card is ‘○△□’ then 1 ETH prize is awarded to the card’s hodler.

If I draw a ‘○○○’ or a ‘ㄱㄱㄱ’ card, can I win 5 ETH prize?

  • No. A card is randomly selected out of 456 cards in each round and if the card has same three consonants, such as ‘○○○’ and also there has been no 5 ETH prize winner by this time then its hodler can receive 5 ETH prize.
  • If 5 ETH prize awarded before, the hodler receives 1 ETH prize.
